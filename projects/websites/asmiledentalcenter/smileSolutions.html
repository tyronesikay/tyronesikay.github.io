Smile Solutions

When you visit our dental practice, Dr. Toquero and her team will perform a thorough assessment of your oral health. As a new patient, you’ll receive a complete evaluation of your teeth and oral tissues. If your bite is misaligned or you have missing teeth, a diagnostic impression may be required. During your examination, we will use digital X-rays and an intraoral camera so that you can see images of your teeth and gums greatly magnified on a computer monitor. This will allow you to better understand the problems we find and our proposed solutions.

Dr. Toquero will always address general and restorative dental issues first, such as cavities or worn fillings, gum disease, or damaged teeth. You’ll be presented with a comprehensive treatment plan detailing services and procedures required to re-establish healthy teeth and gums. We will work with you to develop an affordable program that will address your comfort during dental procedures. Once your oral health is in good condition, Dr. Toquero can talk with you about ways to improve the appearance of your smile through cosmetic dentistry.
