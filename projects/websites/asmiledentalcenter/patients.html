Patient Information and Resources

Our practice is committed to providing you and your family with safe, gentle, and state-of-the-art dental care. We understand that you, or your child, may feel anxious about visiting the dentist; you are not alone, and our practice puts forth an extra effort to make sure that you are comfortable and happy every time you visit our practice. We are sensitive to your needs as a patient; you can always count on receiving individual attention from Dr. Toquero and her team.

Your first visit is all about you– your comfort, your happiness, and your dental health. Your first visit with Dr. Toquero includes:

  • A review of your complete dental and medical history forms.
  • A discussion of any specific concerns or questions you might have about your dental needs.
  • All necessary X-rays of your teeth and mouth.
  • A comprehensive oral exam – including an oral cancer screening, soft tissue and hard tissue exam, making sure your teeth and gums are healthy, and checking for any cavities or early signs of tooth decay.

Once Dr. Toquero has reviewed your dental and medical health history forms, and completed your dental exam, she will provide you with a dental consultation, and information about any additional dental treatments you, or your child, may need. During this time, Dr. Toquero will also answer any questions you may have.

Patient Financing and Insurance Options

The cost of treatment may vary depending on your individual needs and treatment plan. Dr. Toquero and her staff will discuss with you the cost of your treatment, and each of your available payment plan options, so that you are able to make the best choice for you, your smile, and your family.

We accept most forms of payments including, cash, major credit cards, and all PPO insurance providers and offer CareCredit, Chase Health Advance Financing.
