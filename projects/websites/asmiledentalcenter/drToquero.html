Meet Dr. Marilou S. Toquero, DDS.

After Graduating in 1998 from Centro Escolar University, a Dental school abroad, Dr. Marilou Toquero was immediately hired as an associate and practiced dentistry for 2 years. Soon after she immigrated here in the US, Dr. Marilou S. Toquero continued further to complete all the necessary coursework to earn her state board license to practice dentistry in California.

Dr. Toquero started her dental profession as an Associate Dentist in a prosthodontic office, a specialty practice in one of the most desirable areas in the nation, Beverly Hills, Ca., where she was mentored & held to a higher level of standard in the field of Cosmetic Dentistry, that she gratefully and gladly embraced.

In 2008, Dr. Toquero founded her own office focusing in esthetic & cosmetic dentistry. She takes pride in offering the finest quality in patient’s care from investing to the newest and latest technology to finding the most suitable caring, well-educated and friendly team members.

To better serve her patients, Dr. Toquero humbles herself by continuously taking courses to enhance and improve her knowledge in the field of Cosmetic and Esthetic Dentistry. She had taken the UCLA’s Implant training courses and completed the Post Doctoral Degree in Esthetic Dentistry from the same prestigious University.

As charismatic and gentle as she is, Dr. Toquero genuinely dedicates herself to every patient that graces her practice. She treats everyone in high regard and cares for them as her family. Her utmost goal is to deliver an outstanding quality of care and the most pleasing and delightful dental experience for her patients.
