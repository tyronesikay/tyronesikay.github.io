ABOUT US

DengXYZ was established to serve the Filipino-American community in Southern California. When we notice the lack of customer service that some of the well known shipping company provides to their customer, we decided to do something about it. So in 2016 we team up with XYZcargo to form DengXYZcargo, Our mission is to provide excellenct service and treat our customer with utmost respect.
